Frequently Asked Questions

What are standard payment terms for wholesale freight? +
Standard contract execution accepts Irrevocable Letters of Credit (L/C at sight) or Bank Wire Transfers (T/T). Typically, shipments operate on a 30% down-payment configuration, with the remaining 70% balance settled against presentation of the bill of lading documentation pack.
How are quality assays guaranteed across container balances? +
BBO works beside recognized independent testing providers (SGS, Bureau Veritas) to run comprehensive audits. Customers can specify additional pre-shipment extraction criteria to fulfill processing conditions.
What is typical transit fulfillment timing to European / US ports? +
Ocean transit timelines from the Port of Apapa average 18 to 25 days for North European ports, and 28 to 35 days for US Atlantic ports. Custom clearance hand-offs require around 5-7 business working days at departure.
