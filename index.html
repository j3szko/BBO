Frequently Asked Questions

Are these products 100% authentic?

Yes. Every single item is acquired straight from authorized flagship retailers or direct manufacturer distribution pipelines in the US and Seoul. We keep comprehensive order logs to verify compliance maps for every batch.

How can you support both Naira and Dollar payments securely?

Our updated checkout handles dual modes via Paystack. You can choose Naira or Dollars dynamically inside the checkout sheet, and the secure processing system processes the exact value natively on the fly.

Will I be forced to settle surprise custom clearing costs?

No. The local transparent pricing you see contains all international transit metrics, air-freight logistical costs, and port handling adjustments. There are absolutely no hidden fees.

What happens if a product arrives compromised?

Because our cargo handles premium transit lanes, package damage is extremely rare. However, if any seal configuration is broken upon transit arrival, alert us immediately for a fully trackable refund.
