Authenticity & Logistics FAQ

Transparency is our policy.

How do I know this isn't fake?

We completely bypass local middlemen and open markets. Every item is purchased directly from authorized retailers in the US (like Sephora, Ulta) or South Korea (like Olive Young) after you place your order.

Why does it take 3 weeks?

Because it is literally flying from Seoul or New York to Lagos. The wait is your proof of authenticity. We don't hold aging local stock; we source fresh batches upon your request.

Why do the prices change in Naira?

The Naira amount updates according to the current dollar rate. This adjustment lets us buy fresh products straight from global markets seamlessly. The final price you see is comprehensive—there are absolutely no unexpected custom or handling fees added later.

Are there hidden customs fees?

Absolutely not. The Naira price you see on our website is the final landed cost. It covers the product, international air freight, customs clearing, and local logistics. There are completely zero hidden customs or handling fees.

Can't find your holy grail?

If there's a specific US or Korean skincare formula you need that isn't featured here yet, let us know below. We'll source it directly from authorized global retailers for you with zero hidden fees.
