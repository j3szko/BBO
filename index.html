Authenticity & Logistics FAQ

Everything you need to know about our direct sourcing pipeline and quality verification metrics.

How do you verify product texture and consistency?

Counterfeits often have a watered-down, separated, or unusually greasy consistency. Because we completely bypass local open-air markets and middlemen, your products are guaranteed to arrive exactly as the manufacturer originally formulated them, stored in climate-controlled hubs to protect active ingredients.

Do products arrive with intact factory seals and batch codes?

Yes, absolutely. Every single item arrives with completely intact original factory seals and fully verifiable batch tracking codes stamped directly onto the primary packaging. You can trace these numbers using independent manufacturer verification databases to confirm authenticity.

How do I know this isn't fake skincare?

We maintain full structural accountability by purchasing items directly from authorized global retailers in the US and South Korea only after your order is safely generated. We bypass unauthorized liquidators and middle distributors entirely, guaranteeing 100% genuine formulations.

Are there any hidden customs or handling fees on arrival?

None at all. The price calculated inside our single-page secure checkout model covers every logistical link—including transatlantic air freight clearance fees and processing tariffs. There are absolutely no delivery surcharges requested at your doorstep.
